# FinWise
### Should you actually buy that?

FinWise is the AI financial anchor that sits between you and every impulse purchase. Paste a product link, get an honest verdict based on your real financial situation — then watch it find the same thing cheaper somewhere else.

No more guessing. No more buyer's remorse.

---

## How it works

1. **Paste a link** to anything you're about to buy
2. **Get a verdict** — buy, wait, or skip — grounded in your income, savings goals, and monthly budget
3. **See cheaper options** — FinWise searches live prices across retailers and only shows you results that actually save money

---

## Why it matters

Every budgeting app tells you what you already spent. FinWise catches you before you spend — and when you do, it makes sure you're getting the best deal.
